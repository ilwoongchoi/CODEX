# 1. Biophysical Pathophysiology

The pathology of Feather Plucking cannot be understood solely through behavior. It represents a failure of physics. Specifically, the mechanism of Mechanoreceptor Starvation initiates a cascade of cellular dysfunction.

Parrots are flock animals. They require 4-6 hours of physical touch (Allogrooming) daily to regulate cortisol. Isolation removes this regulatory input. The bird plucks its own feathers to stimulate the mechanoreceptors (Endorphins) that are starving for touch. It is auto-stimulation of a deprivation state.
