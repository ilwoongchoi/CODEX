  1. Biophysical Pathophysiology

The pathology of Bone as a Piezoelectric Dielectric Shield cannot be understood solely through biochemistry. It represents a failure of physics. Specifically, the mechanism of Dielectric Collapse initiates a cascade of cellular dysfunction.

Bone is not merely structural; it is a Faraday Cage. The conventional pharmacological approach often fails because it addresses the downstream chemical receptor without correcting the upstream physical environment.
